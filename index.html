About the Magazine

Sustainable Farming is a half-yearly magazine dedicated to advancing knowledge, innovation, and dialogue in agriculture and allied sciences. It provides a platform for scientists, academicians, extension workers, policymakers, students, and progressive farmers to share insights that bridge traditional practices with modern technologies.

By presenting research articles, success stories, field experiences, and policy perspectives, the magazine seeks to inspire sustainable agricultural growth and ensure food, nutrition, and livelihood security for present and future generations.

Publisher: College of Agriculture, JNKVV, Ganj Basoda, MP, India – 464221

Aim

The aim of Sustainable Farming is to disseminate credible and research-based knowledge that promotes eco-friendly, economically viable, and socially acceptable agricultural practices. The magazine highlights innovative technologies, encourages collaboration among stakeholders, addresses climate change and resource challenges, and promotes agribusiness opportunities for rural youth.
